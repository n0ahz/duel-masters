Cards were released in Expansion Sets.

A block is a series of Duel Masters Expansion sets.

They are often grouped together in groups of 3, 4 or 5 sets, with a major 120-card set, and 2-4 smaller 60-card sets.

They were sold in small packs called Booster Packs. Also various special Starter Packs, Theme Decks etc were sold which contained more cards than the Booster Packs.
